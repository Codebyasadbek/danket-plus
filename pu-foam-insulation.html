THE HEAT IN DUBAI POSES A REAL CHALLENGE FOR ANY ROOF

Dubai's climate creates constant thermal stress that reduces energy efficiency and accelerates roof ageing.

  • Roof surfaces reach 70–80°C

  • Heat transfers into the building

  • Higher cooling demand and AC costs

  • Daily thermal movement stresses roof joints

SPF forms a seamless thermal barrier that reduces heat transfer and cooling demand.

WHERE WE APPLY SPF INSULATION

The most popular

ROOF INSULATION — COMMERCIAL &
RESIDENTIAL

The primary application of SPF in Dubai. Applied to flat roofs, sloped roofs and exposed roof slabs — on commercial buildings, apartment blocks, mixed-use developments and residential properties. Can be applied internally or externally. Works over existing substrates without full demolition, assessed after inspection.

Typical scope: 500–5,000 m².

VILLAS AND RESIDENTIAL BUILDINGS

Applied to roof slabs, cavities and pitched sections. Reduces heat gain through the roof, lowers AC costs and improves indoor comfort on upper floors. In Dubai's climate, an insulated roof can significantly reduce cooling load and stabilise temperatures in occupied spaces.

Applied in 1–3 days depending on scope.

WAREHOUSES, FACTORIES AND LOGISTICS FACILITIES

Large-span metal or concrete structures — warehouses, factories, logistics centres and commercial buildings — with high exposed roof surface areas. SPF applied to the underside of roof panels or external roof surfaces. Eliminates heat retention, removes condensation at panel joints.

Output: up to 800–1,000 m² per shift under suitable conditions.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

Seamless layer — no joints, no cold bridges

Thickness: 30–150 mm (matched to heat load and project requirements)

Adheres directly to concrete, metal, wood — no mechanical fixing required

  • Foam density 30–60 kg/m³
  • Thermal conductivity ~0.020–0.028 W/m·K
  • Operating range -60°C to +100°C
  • Layer per pass 10–30 mm
  • UV protection required for outdoor use

SOLUTIONS USED IN EUROPE OR CIS COUNTRIES OFTEN FAIL IN DUBAI

Constant heat, intense UV exposure and humidity create conditions where standard systems degrade faster. We select and configure polyurethane foam systems specifically for UAE operating conditions.
