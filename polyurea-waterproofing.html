WHY POLYUREA FOR DUBAI

Polyurea waterproofing is designed to perform under Dubai's extreme climate, where constant heat, UV exposure and humidity place continuous stress on roofing systems.

  • Constant heat and UV exposure

    Accelerates ageing of conventional waterproofing materials.

  • Daily thermal movement

    Roof structures expand and contract, stressing joints and details.

  • Vulnerable joints & penetrations

    Corners, pipe entries and transitions are where most failures begin.

  • Humidity and moisture ingress

    Water penetrates through weak points and gradually damages the structure.

  • Seamless protective membrane

    Polyurea creates a continuous waterproof layer with no joints or overlaps.

APPLICATION — HOW THE SYSTEM IS BUILT

The service life of waterproofing is not defined after completion — it is formed during execution.

01

Inspection

We assess the substrate, identify risk zones and define the scope.

02

System selection

Polyurea, SPF or combined — matched to the structure and UAE climate conditions.

03

Application

Surface preparation, detail treatment, spray application with layer thickness control.

04

Handover

Final inspection, material certificates and as-built documentation.

Material & Surface Preparation

Material compliance, storage conditions and readiness are verified before work begins. The surface is cleaned, repaired, primed and prepared for adhesion.

Detail Treatment

All corners, joints, penetrations and transitions are treated individually before the main coat is applied.

Controlled Application

Layer thickness and uniformity are monitored throughout the application process. Each stage is documented.

Inspection & Handover

Coating continuity is verified. Material certificates and as-built documentation are provided on completion.
