UAE conditions

Why Dubai requires a different approach

Waterproofing in Dubai operates under constant thermal and environmental stress. Heat, UV radiation, humidity and thermal movement accelerate coating ageing and increase the risk of leaks, especially around joints and penetrations.

  • Heat Roof surfaces reach extreme temperatures.
  • Humidity Moisture penetrates slabs and walls.
  • UV Exposure Standard coatings degrade faster.
  • Thermal Movement Daily expansion stresses joints and seams.
  • AC Load Constant cooling increases roof temperature gradients.
  • Roofs & Joints The most common locations where leaks begin.

Risk zones

Why details matter

Most waterproofing failures do not begin on open roof surfaces. They start at construction details where different materials meet, movement occurs or service penetrations interrupt the membrane. These areas require reinforced treatment and careful application.

Vulnerable points include:

  • Structural joints & connections
  • Service entry points and pipe penetrations
  • Terraces and balconies
  • Areas around pools
  • Seams and transitions between materials

  • Leaks most often appear in these zones — not in the middle of flat surfaces.

    Details determine how the system performs in operation.

Failure progression

How roof problems develop

Building envelope problems usually develop gradually — long before visible leaks appear.

Early signs:

  • Surface cracking and joint movement
  • Condensation around AC and service penetrations
  • Local damp areas or staining

These signs indicate that the roof system is already under stress. Without timely maintenance, insulation performance decreases and moisture begins to penetrate the structure.

As the system deteriorates, joints open, protective layers wear down and thermal performance declines. Small defects become larger and repair costs increase over time.

In Dubai, constant heat, UV exposure and daily thermal movement accelerate this process. Roof systems must resist both moisture and extreme environmental conditions to maintain long-term performance.

Our solutions

Core services

Polyurea waterproofing

Spray-applied seamless membrane. No seams, no joints, no weak points. Used for roofs, pools, foundations, terraces and underground structures. Cures in minutes, elastic under thermal movement, resistant to UAE heat and UV.

More detailed

SPF insulation (spray polyurethane foam)

Spray-applied insulation that bonds directly to the substrate and covers the entire surface as one continuous layer. Reduces roof heat load, stabilises indoor temperature, lowers AC costs. Applied to roofs, walls and ceilings — including complex geometry.

More detailed

SPF + Polyurea — combined roofing system

SPF as the insulation base, polyurea as the waterproofing membrane on top. The most complete solution for exposed roofs in Dubai: thermal protection and seamless waterproofing in a single system.

    SPF applied to roof slabs, cavities and pitched sections. Reduces heat ingress, stabilises indoor temperature, lowers AC load. Combined with polyurea waterproofing where required.

  • Foundation Waterproofing
